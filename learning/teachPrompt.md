1. 20-Hour Mastery Plan
Prompt: “I need to learn [topic] fast. Build me a
20-hour plan focused on the 20% that drives 80% of results. Break it into 10 two-hour sessions with the best resources and a 15-minute review at each end.”
2. One-Page Cheat Sheet
Prompt: “Summarize the key concepts of [topic] on a single page. Use bullet points, diagrams, and examples so I can review it in 5 minutes.”
3. Progressive Quiz Drill
Prompt: “I just studied [topic]. Quiz me until | break: give 10 progressively harder questions.
Grade each answer and explain what I missed.”
4. Learning Ladder
Prompt: “Break [topic] into 5 levels from beginner to advanced. Show clear milestones, practice tasks, and how to progress step-by-step.”
5. Top Resources Guide
Prompt: “Find the best 5 resources (books, videos, courses) for [topic]. Explain why each accelerates learning and how to use them.”
6. Feynman Technique Loop
Prompt: “Teach [topic] simply like I’m 12. Then let me re-explain it back-point out gaps, reteach misses, and repeat until I own it.”
